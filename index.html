Thanks for stopping by. I've always wanted to build a simple website where anyone who is interested can check out my work and learn a little about me. It will be a constant work in progress, so if you are really bored, be sure to come back and check in on me.

  • Some things about me...

  • 1987    Born and raised in Charlotte, NC
  • 2006    Enrolled at The University of Georgia (go dawgs)
  • 2008    Majored in business / finance
  • 2010    Moved to Atlanta to work for an investment bank
  • 2012    Quit job and moved to Jackson, Wyoming looking for change and to learn how to ski
  • 2015    Unable to escape my love of excel and double monitors, took job at brokerage firm and later a small commuity bank (but still got to ski!)
  • 2016    Realizing my propensity for automation using Excel/VBA, began exploring the world of coding. Taught myself entry-level HTML, CSS and some Javascript
  • 2017    Took another leap of faith, this time with my career, and enrolled in a coding bootcamp. A few months later moved back to Charlotte, where I spent three months attending an immersive frontend web development program at The Iron Yard
  • 2017    Landed my first job as a junior software developer at Ekos Brewmaster!
